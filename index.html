About Us

ABC Driving School has been a trusted name in the Payyoli and Kozhikode region for over 40 years. We focus deeply on road safety, confidence building, and defensive driver awareness.

With modern dual-clutch training vehicles and certified, patient instructors, we ensure top-quality road training for learners of all ages and experience levels.
