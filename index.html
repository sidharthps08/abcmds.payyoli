About Us

ABC Driving School has been a trusted name in the Payyoli and Kozhikode region for over 40 years. We focus on road safety, confidence building, and driver awareness. With modern dual-clutch vehicles and certified instructors, we ensure top-quality training for learners of all ages.

  • Advanced Driving Techniques
  • Daily & Weekend Classes
  • Defensive Driving Course
  • Road Test Preparation (Kerala MVD)
  • Night & Highway Driving Sessions
  • Special sessions for Senior Citizens
  • Female-Friendly Training Options
  • Computer Training for Learner Licence
  • RTO Related Works
